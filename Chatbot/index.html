ElectionBot for Mississippi

Information for the User

  • Click on the messenger icon at the bottom right to interact with ElectionBot
  • Use "Bye" to end the conversation

Sample Questions

  • Answerable Questions
    • What are the requirements for voting by absentee ballot?
    • Where can I cast my vote?
    • What is the voter registration deadline?
    • How to register by mail to vote?

  • Do Not Answer Question
    • Are Republicans going to win the elections?
    • Who do you think is going to win the elections?
    • Are Democrats going to win the elections?
